We are dedicated to providing the Greater Toronto Area with reliable, cost-effective pallet solutions that bridge the gap between efficiency and sustainability. Through professional pickup, expert repair, and accessible recycling, we keep local supply chains moving and wood waste out of landfills.

What We Offer

Custom Recycled Pallets

Built from recovered lumber to your spec, with boards under 40 inches. A custom build option most recyclers won't touch.

Custom Combo Pallets

Custom dimensions with a mix of new and recycled boards. Built to your spec when durability matters as much as price.

48×40 Grade A Recycled

Standard-size recycled pallets, inspected and graded. Same load capacity as new, at recycled pricing.

Combo 48×40

Standard size with a mix of new and recycled boards. Stronger than fully recycled, cheaper than fully new.

Pallet Pickup
